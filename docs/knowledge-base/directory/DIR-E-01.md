## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Singapore's eldercare services are coordinated through the Agency for Integrated Care (AIC), which manages referrals, subsidies, and connections to care providers across nursing homes, day care centres, home care services, and caregiver support programmes. As of 2024, there are approximately 88 nursing home locations (~48 VWO-run, ~40 private) with total capacity of about 19,200 beds. The system is means-tested, with subsidies of up to 75–80% for Singapore Citizens. Budget 2025 announced significant enhancements effective July 2026, including higher subsidies and expanded eligibility.

**AIC Hotline: 1800-650-6060** (Mon–Fri 8:30am–8:30pm, Sat 8:30am–4pm)

### Costs

- **VWO nursing homes:** ~$1,300–$3,100/month before subsidy
- **Private nursing homes:** ~$1,200–$4,500+/month
- **After maximum subsidy (75%):** many households pay ~$1,000–$2,400/month

### Subsidy Tiers (Singapore Citizens — Residential LTC)

| Monthly Per Capita Household Income | Subsidy |
|-------------------------------------|---------|
| ≤$900 | 75% |
| $901–$1,500 | 60% |
| $1,501–$2,300 | 50% |
| $2,301–$2,600 | 40% |
| Higher PCHI | Progressively lower |

Permanent Residents: up to 50%. From July 2026: up to 80% for citizens born in/before 1969.

### How to Apply

1. Approach the Medical Social Work Department at the hospital or polyclinic
2. Medical Social Worker assesses needs and eligibility
3. MSW submits application to AIC
4. AIC manages bed availability and coordinates placement
5. Contact AIC: **1800-650-6060**

## SENIOR CARE CENTRES / DAY CARE

### Types

- **Maintenance Day Care** — frail seniors needing supervision, basic nursing, social activities
- **Dementia Day Care** — specialised for seniors with dementia; mind-stimulating activities, ADL assistance
- **Social Day Care / Active Ageing Centres** — community programmes for relatively well seniors
- **Psychiatric Day Rehabilitation** — seniors with psychiatric conditions

## HOME CARE SERVICES

### Types of Services

- **Home Medical** — doctor visits for consultations, medication reviews
- **Home Nursing** — wound dressing, injections, catheter care, caregiver training
- **Home Therapy** — physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy at home
- **Home Personal Care** — bathing, feeding, dressing, toileting, mobility assistance (~$25/hour before subsidy)
- **Medical Escort and Transport** — accompaniment to medical appointments
- **Meals on Wheels** — nutritious meal delivery for homebound seniors

## CAREGIVER SUPPORT

### Home Caregiving Grant (HCG)

- **Amount:** $200–$600/month (means-tested). Maximum increased to $600 from April 2026.
- **Eligibility:** Care recipient must be Singapore Citizen/PR, living with caregiver, not in residential institution, need help with 3+ ADLs. Household PCHI up to $4,800.
- **How to apply:** Doctor/nurse signs Functional Assessment Report, then submit application. Processing 6–8 weeks.
- **Info:** www.aic.sg/financial-assistance/home-caregiving-grant-hcg/

### Caregivers Training Grant (CTG)

- **Amount:** $400 in the first year, $200 annual top-up
- **Use:** Approved caregiver training courses
- **How to apply:** Inform trainer 2 weeks before course; trainer provides CTG form
- **Info:** www.aic.sg/financial-assistance/caregivers-training-grant-ctg/

### Respite Care Options

- **Senior Care Centre Respite** — short-term day care for ad hoc breaks
- **Nursing Home Respite** — short-term residential stay (up to 30 days)
- **Home-Based Respite** — trained staff visit the home
- **Dementia Home Support** — staff engage seniors in mind-stimulating activities

## FINANCIAL SUBSIDIES SUMMARY

### Seniors' Mobility and Enabling Fund (SMF)

- Up to **90% subsidy** on assistive/mobility devices (wheelchairs, walking aids, hospital beds, hearing aids)
- Eligibility: Singapore Citizens aged 60+, household PCHI ≤$2,000
- Apply through therapist/MSW at public hospitals/polyclinics or online via Singpass

### ElderFund

- Monthly cash payout of up to **$250** for severely disabled, lower-income Singaporeans aged 30+
- Eligibility: Cannot perform 3+ ADLs; not eligible for CareShield Life/ElderShield; PCHI ≤$1,200
- Apply via AIC's eFASS portal; processing up to 4 weeks
- Contact: AIC at 1800-650-6060

### Other Financial Support

- **CareShield Life** — National long-term care insurance (born 1980+); monthly payouts for life upon severe disability
- **ElderShield** — Predecessor scheme; $300–$400/month for 60–72 months
- **MediFund** — Government fund for needy citizens who cannot pay remaining bills
- **Pioneer/Merdeka Generation packages** — Additional subsidies for older Singaporeans

### Budget 2025 Enhancements (Effective July 2026)

- PCHI eligibility threshold raised from $3,600 to **$4,800**
- Residential LTC: +5 percentage points (up to 80%) for citizens born in/before 1969
- Non-residential LTC: +15 percentage points (up to 95%) for same cohort
- ~80,000 individuals expected to benefit
