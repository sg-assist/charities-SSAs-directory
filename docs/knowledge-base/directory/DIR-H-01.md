## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Singapore's healthcare charity landscape includes major hospital foundations, disease-specific organisations, and a well-developed palliative care network. Healthcare subsidies follow a "3M" framework — MediSave, MediShield Life, and MediFund — with additional schemes like CHAS for primary care. Palliative care is provided through home hospice, inpatient hospice, and day hospice services, with subsidies of up to 80% for Singapore Citizens.

### Palliative Care Subsidies

- Singapore Citizens: up to **80%**; PRs: up to **50%**

- Equipment Rental Subsidies: at least 50% for patients with ≤1 year prognosis

## COMMUNITY HEALTH SCHEMES

### CHAS (Community Health Assist Scheme)

All Singapore Citizens eligible. Subsidies at GP and dental clinics.

| Tier | Income (PCHI/month) | Common Illness Subsidy |
|------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Blue | ≤$1,500 | Up to $18.50/visit |
| Orange | $1,501–$2,300 | Up to $10.00/visit |
| Green | >$2,300 | N/A for common illness |
| Pioneer Generation | Any | Up to $28.50/visit |
| Merdeka Generation | Any | Up to $23.50/visit |

**Chronic condition subsidies** range from $28–$135/visit depending on tier and complexity.

- Hotline: **1800-275-2427** (1800-ASK-CHAS)
- Website: www.chas.sg
- Application: chas.moh.gov.sg

### MediFund

Safety net for citizens who cannot afford bills after subsidies, MediShield Life, and MediSave.

- Applied through Medical Social Worker at MediFund-approved public institutions
- Variants: MediFund (general), MediFund Silver (65+), MediFund Junior (≤18)
- No fixed amounts — decided by independent committee at each institution

### MediShield Life

Universal health insurance for all citizens and PRs.

- Annual claim limit: **$200,000** (from April 2025)
- No lifetime claim limit
- Premium subsidies: up to 60% for lower/middle-income
- New outpatient deductible: $500/year from January 2026

### Hospital Subsidies (Public Hospitals, B2/C Wards)

- Singapore Citizens: **50–80%** (means-tested)
- PRs: up to 50%
- Automatic based on means test — no application required

## MEDICAL SOCIAL WORK

Medical Social Workers (MSWs) are based in all public hospitals and polyclinics. They provide:
- Financial counselling and subsidy navigation
- MediFund applications
- Emotional and psychosocial support
- Discharge planning and community resource connections

**How to access:** Through doctor's referral, or approach the hospital's Medical Social Services department directly.
