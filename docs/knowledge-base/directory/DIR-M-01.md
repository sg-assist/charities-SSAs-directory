## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Singapore's mental health services are anchored by the Institute of Mental Health (IMH) as the tertiary psychiatric hospital, supported by a network of community mental health teams (CREST and COMIT), voluntary welfare organisations, and a growing set of 24/7 helplines. The national mindline 1771, launched in June 2025, is Singapore's first national mental health helpline and textline, operating 24/7.

**Critical helpline updates:**
- **Samaritans of Singapore (SOS): 1767** — 24/7 suicide prevention
- **national mindline 1771** — 24/7 mental health support (launched June 2025)
- **IMH Mental Health Helpline 6389 2222** — RETIRED 18 June 2025 (replaced by mindline 1771)
- **National CARE Hotline 1800-202-6868** — CEASED 31 Dec 2022

## INSTITUTE OF MENTAL HEALTH (IMH)

Singapore's only tertiary psychiatric hospital. 50+ wards, ~2,010 inpatient beds, 7 specialist outpatient clinics, plus satellite clinics and community mental health programmes.

- **Address:** 10 Buangkok View, S539747 (near Buangkok MRT)
- **General Enquiries:** **6389 2000** (24 hours)
- **Website:** imh.com.sg

### Key departments

- Department of General Psychiatry
- Department of Mood and Anxiety Disorders
- Department of Psychosis (including Early Psychosis Intervention Programme — EPIP)
- Department of Developmental Psychiatry (Child Guidance Clinic)
- Department of Geriatric Psychiatry
- Department of Addiction Medicine (National Addictions Management Service — NAMS)
- Department of Forensic Psychiatry
- Department of Psychological Medicine

### Community-based services through IMH

- **CHAT** — Community Health Assessment Team (youth mental health, ages 16–30)
- **MCT** — Mobile Crisis Team
- **AIMH** — Assisted Independence for Mental Health
- **REACH** — Response Early Intervention and Assessment in Community Mental Health (for schools)
- **CREST** — Community Resource, Engagement and Support Team

### How to access

- **Non-emergency:** GP or polyclinic referral for appointments
- **Crisis:** Walk-in Emergency Services (24/7)
- **Appointments:** Main line 6389 2000
- **Telemedicine:** Phone/video consultation available

## CHAT — YOUTH MENTAL HEALTH (ages 16–30)

CHAT (Centre of Excellence for Youth Mental Health) is IMH's free and confidential mental health assessment service for young people.

- **Hub address:** *SCAPE, 2 Orchard Link, #04-07, S237978
- **Phone:** **6493 6500 / 6501**
- **Hours:** Tue–Sat 12pm–9pm (excl. PH); weekend and after-hours assessments also available
- **Website:** chat.mentalhealth.sg | imh.com.sg/CHAT
- **Services:** Free assessments (3 As — Awareness, Access, Assessment); webCHAT e-mental health; outreach; referrals to specialist clinics or school/community counsellors
- **Access:** Online booking, phone, or walk-in

As of end-March 2024, CHAT has supported 7,600+ youth — 79.2% of those assessed had mental health issues, with 61.5% referred to specialist clinics.

## COMMUNITY MENTAL HEALTH — CREST & COMIT

Both programmes are part of the **Community Mental Health Masterplan** led by AIC with MOH. **Both are fully subsidised by government (free to clients).** Over 100 community mental health teams operate islandwide.

**AIC Infoline: 1800-650-6060** | aic.sg

### CREST — Community Resource, Engagement and Support Team

Community outreach team providing screening, emotional support, befriending, psychoeducation, and links to further care. Focus: depression, dementia, and mental health conditions. Variants: CREST (general/seniors) and CREST-Youth.

**Operators include:**
- **Fei Yue Community Services** — Ang Mo Kio–Hougang, Hougang SMC, Punggol, Buona Vista, Queenstown, Bukit Timah, Clementi, Ulu Pandan, Bukit Panjang SMC, Cashew, Zhenghua, Brickland, Choa Chu Kang, Limbang, Yew Tee, Paya Lebar, Serangoon
- **REACH Community Services** — Bukit Gombak, Hong Kah North
- **TOUCH Community Services** — Cheng San–Seletar
- **Club HEAL** — multiple zones
- **CARE Singapore** — Pasir Ris, Tampines East (CREST-Youth)
- **AWWA** — seniors CREST
- Others: Montfort Care, Allkin, Filos, THK

### COMIT — Community Intervention Team

Higher-tier community service providing psychosocial interventions (CBT, case management, psychoeducation) for individuals with moderate mental health needs. Bridges primary care/GP Partners and specialist tertiary psychiatric services.

**Operators:** Fei Yue, REACH, TOUCH, Club HEAL, SAMH, AWWA, Allkin, and others — operating islandwide.

**Access:** Referral via GP, polyclinic, CREST, hospital, school counsellor, or self-referral via AIC.

## HOW TO ACCESS HELP

1. **In crisis (immediate):** Call **1767** (SOS) or **1771** (mindline) or **999** (Police). Walk into any public hospital Emergency Department.
2. **Non-urgent assessment:** Visit a GP or polyclinic for referral. Subsidised rates apply.
3. **Youth (16–30):** CHAT at *SCAPE — free assessment, no referral needed.
4. **Community support:** Call **AIC 1800-650-6060** for CREST/COMIT in your area.
5. **Caregivers:** Contact **CAL 6460 4400**.
6. **Women:** AWARE Women's Helpline **1800-777-5555**.
