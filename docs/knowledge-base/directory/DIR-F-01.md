## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Singapore's family services are delivered primarily through ~40 Family Service Centres (FSCs) across all constituencies, supported by 20+ Social Service Offices (SSOs) for financial assistance applications. The system covers family counselling, financial aid (ComCare), family violence intervention, and children/youth protection. This document lists every major provider with addresses and contact details.

**ComCare Hotline: 1800-222-0000** | **National Anti-Violence Helpline: 1800-777-0000** (24 hours)

## FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE SCHEMES

### ComCare Short-to-Medium-Term Assistance (SMTA)

Temporary financial help for lower-income individuals/families who are temporarily unable to work, job-seeking, or earning low income.

- **Eligibility:** Monthly household income ≤$1,900 or per capita income ≤$650
- **Amount:** Cash assistance tailored to household needs (varies by size and circumstances)
- **Duration:** Typically 3–6 months, renewable
- **Additional support:** Utility bills, rental, S&CC assistance; job referrals
- **Apply at:** Nearest SSO or call **1800-222-0000**
- Higher rates from 1 April 2025

### ComCare Long-Term Assistance (LTA)

Ongoing support for those permanently unable to work due to old age, illness, or disability.

- **Eligibility:** Singapore Citizen/PR; permanently unable to work; little/no family support
- **Amount (from April 2025):** 1-person household ~$760/month (up from $640). Scales by household size.
- **Apply at:** Nearest SSO or call **1800-222-0000**

### CDC Vouchers (FY2025)

Digital vouchers for all Singaporean households — no income test required.

- **Amount:** $800 total ($500 from May 2025 + $300 from Jan 2026)
- **Eligibility:** Any household with ≥1 Singapore Citizen
- **Claim at:** go.gov.sg/cdcv (login with Singpass)
- **Valid until:** 31 December 2026
- **Usable at:** Participating supermarkets, hawkers, heartland merchants
